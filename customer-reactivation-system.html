How to run this

Day 1–3: Send Message 1 to ~40–50 people per day. Tap “Send M1” — WhatsApp opens with the text ready, you just press send. Mark replies as they come in.

Day 4–8: Send Message 2 (the offer) to everyone who got M1 — the filter “Due next message” shows exactly who's next.

Day 7–11: Message 3 only to people who haven't replied or ordered. Then stop — don't chase further.

When someone orders: tap “Ordered”. Your conversion rate shows at the top — 3–8% is normal for a warm list.

Opt-outs: if anyone says stop, tap “Opt out” — they're excluded from everything after. This keeps you compliant with UK marketing rules.

Monthly after that: reuse Message 2 with a new offer (new stock, seasonal, Eid/Ramadan) — max 2–3 messages a month per customer.
